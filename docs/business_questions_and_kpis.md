# Business Questions & KPI Overview

## Context

PanPac is a renewables / forestry company:
- Grows and harvests radiata pine in NZ.
- Runs mills to produce timber / pulp products.
- Ships products to domestic and export customers.
- Cares about safety, sustainability, and profitability.

## Core Business Questions

1. **Operations / Mills**
   - How is each mill performing in terms of output, yield, and downtime?
   - Which sites are driving most of the production bottlenecks?

2. **Supply Chain / Logistics**
   - Are we shipping on time and in full (OTIF) to key customers?
   - Which routes or ports have the highest delay / disruption risk?

3. **Finance**
   - How are revenue, gross margin, and EBITDA trending by product and region?
   - Are we meeting budget / forecast at business unit level?

4. **Sustainability / Safety**
   - What is our rate of safety incidents per million hours worked?
   - How do energy usage and emissions intensity trend over time?

## Initial KPI List (high level)

- **Production Yield %** = Output volume / Input log volume
- **Mill Throughput** = m³ produced per day per mill
- **Downtime %** = Downtime hours / Total scheduled hours
- **On-Time In-Full (OTIF) %** = Shipments delivered on time and complete / Total shipments
- **Average Lead Time (days)** = Delivery date – Order date
- **Revenue** (NZD) by month, product, region
- **Gross Margin %** = (Revenue – Direct cost) / Revenue
- **EBITDA Margin %** (simplified)
- **Safety Incidents per 1M hours**
- **Energy Intensity** = kWh per m³ produced (optional)
