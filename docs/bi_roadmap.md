# PanPac BI Roadmap (Portfolio Version)

## Phase 1 – Foundations (This project)

- Define business questions and KPIs.
- Design a semantic model across Operations, Supply Chain, and Finance.
- Build a single source of truth in DuckDB.
- Create a core dashboard for executives and operations leaders.
- Document KPI definitions and data governance basics.

## Phase 2 – Enablement & Adoption (Conceptual)

- Introduce role-based views for:
  - Mill managers
  - Supply chain planners
  - Finance business partners
- Run workshops and training for self-service analytics.
- Establish a BI community of practice and feedback loops.

## Phase 3 – Automation & AI Assist (This project, demo version)

- Deploy an "Insight Agent" that:
  - Monitors key KPIs daily.
  - Detects anomalies and threshold breaches.
  - Generates a plain-language daily summary for leaders.

## Phase 4 – Enterprise-Scale (Future concept)

- Integrate with SAP / SAC or enterprise data warehouse.
- Implement row-level security and certified datasets.
- Extend AI capabilities with governed natural-language querying.
